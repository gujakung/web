Three tribes of Cryptures were living peacefully in their designated territory.

But one day,members of each tribes started to disappear and tribes began to suspect each other.

Number of missing Cryptures continued to increase and conflict between each tribes got intensified.

It didn't take long for the conflict to become a quarrel, and when the moment war was about to break out,

Mysterious monsters appeared and started to surround cryptures' troops.

Three tribes figured it out that those monsters are responsible for the disappearance of members.

Tribes realized that they have to form an alliance to defend themselves from monsters.
  • FAQ 01.
    What is Random Crypture Defense?

    Random Crypture Defense is P2E tower defense sort of game.

    Every Crypture is assigned with elements depending on their RGB Color.

    You can Collect your own unique Cryptures and build up strategies.

    Winner will get rewarded by token and it can be used to upgrade or get new Cryptures

  • FAQ 02.
